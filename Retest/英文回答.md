## 毕业设计

CN：论文题为《基于机器学习的华西雨屏区土壤容重空间分布特征研究》，属于环境科学与工程专业。华西雨屏区是我国重要生态屏障，但面临水土流失和土地退化问题。土壤容重是评估土壤质量的关键，影响植被生长和水分渗透。传统测量方法费时且覆盖范围有限。为此，我结合机器学习、遥感数据和环境变量，研究该区土壤容重分布及其驱动因素，为生态恢复提供科学依据。

我采集了 24 个站点的土壤容重数据，整合降雨、地形、植被等环境因子，构建数据库。使用随机森林和 XGBoost 模型，筛选关键变量并预测分布特征。当前，我正分析海拔、坡度、降雨对土壤容重的影响，以揭示分布规律。研究建立了土壤容重与环境因子的数据库，明确了主要驱动因素。结果将支持土壤质量评估和生态修复。创新点在于首次应用机器学习实现大范围高效预测。

EN：The thesis, titled "Study on the Spatial Distribution Characteristics of Soil Bulk Density in the Rain-Shelter Area of Western China Based on Machine Learning," pertains to the field of Environmental Science and Engineering. The rain-shelter area in western China is a vital ecological barrier in the country, yet it faces significant challenges such as soil erosion and land degradation. Soil bulk density, a critical indicator for evaluating soil quality, affects vegetation growth and water infiltration. Traditional measurement approaches are time-intensive and limited in spatial scope. To overcome these limitations, I combined machine learning techniques with remote sensing data and environmental variables to explore the spatial distribution of soil bulk density and its driving factors in this region, thereby providing a scientific foundation for ecological restoration.

I gathered soil bulk density data from 24 sites and integrated environmental factors—including precipitation, topography, and vegetation—to create a comprehensive database. By employing Random Forest and XGBoost models, I identified key variables and predicted the spatial distribution patterns. Currently, I am examining the effects of altitude, slope, and precipitation on soil bulk density to uncover its distribution 规律 (patterns). The study established a database linking soil bulk density with environmental factors and elucidated the primary driving forces. These findings will facilitate soil quality assessments and ecological restoration initiatives. The innovation of this research lies in its pioneering use of machine learning to achieve efficient, large-scale predictions in this region.
